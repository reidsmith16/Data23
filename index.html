Welcome to Florida Atlantic University

About FAU

Florida Atlantic University (FAU) is a public research university located in Boca Raton, Florida. It was founded in 1961 and has since grown to become a prominent institution in the state.

Campuses

FAU has multiple campuses, including the main campus in Boca Raton, as well as campuses in Davie, Fort Lauderdale, and Jupiter. Each campus offers a unique academic and social experience.

Academics

FAU offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across various disciplines, including business, engineering, health sciences, arts, and more. The university is known for its research contributions and innovative programs.

Sports

The FAU Owls compete in NCAA Division I sports and have a strong athletic program. Football, basketball, and baseball are among the popular sports at the university.
